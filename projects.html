Welcome to the official website of Rayfield Community Projects

This platform helps organize and manage community projects efficiently.

Some of the major projects managed by RCP includes:

Emergency Relief

To ensure adequate provision of relief materials to those displaced as a result of the religious crisis and flooding in the community

Mother and Child Care

Our mission is to ensure that adequate health care facilities are provided across the various communities in the Rayfield area and that proper medication and services are rendered at discounted rates.

Childhood Education

To ensure that every child gets proper education and educational materials

Youth Development

Ensuring that youths at the Rayfield Community and its environments are engaged in gainful activities by empowering them with proper skills.

Elder Care

Ensuring that the care of the elderly are priotized and retirement homes are adequately provided and maintained
